## Slide 5: How It Works
**Simple 4-Step Process**

1. **Input Soil Parameters**
   - Soil type (clay, sandy, loam, etc.)
   - Moisture level (0-100%)
   - Organic content (0-10%)
   - pH level (4-10)

2. **AI Analysis**
   - Algorithm processes 4 key parameters
   - Cross-references with crop database
   - Considers multiple soil factors simultaneously

3. **Get Recommendations**
   - Up to 6 personalized crop suggestions
   - Suitability ratings (Excellent/Good/Fair)
   - Detailed benefits and growing tips

4. **Implement & Track**
   - Apply recommendations in the field
   - Track results for continuous improvement
